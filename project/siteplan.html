Site Name

Samuel Innocent - Data & Dev Portfolio

I chose this name because it is my personal professional brand. As a Data Scientist and Web Developer, my name is the "product." It clearly tells recruiters exactly who I am and what I do immediately upon landing on the page.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a professional portfolio to showcase my technical skills and specific data projects. The site will be divided into three main sections:

  • About Me: A professional biography highlighting my background in Nigeria and my dual focus on Data Science and Dynamic Web Fundamentals.
  • Project Showcase: A detailed display of my key projects, specifically the "Telco Customer Churn & CLV Predictor" app and my Python-based "Energy Analysis" project.
  • Contact: A functional area for potential employers to connect with me, including links to my LinkedIn and GitHub.

Scenarios

Questions my target audience (Recruiters & Employers) might ask:

  • "I see Samuel lists 'Data Science' on his resume; where can I see code examples of his Python Energy Analysis?"
  • "Does Samuel have any experience combining data backend logic with a frontend web interface?"

Color Schema

I selected a professional, high-contrast palette. "Midnight Blue" conveys authority and intelligence (common in tech/data), while "Emerald Green" provides a fresh accent representing growth and energy (tying into my Energy Analysis project).
